My Cricket Journey

From Childhood Passion to University Champion

Cricket has always held a special place in my life. Growing up in Pakistan, it was the first sport most children learned—and I was no exception. While football is gaining popularity, cricket remains my passion.

I've proudly represented my school, college, and now university teams over the years. At HKUST, I've been part of the university cricket team for the past two years.

The university not only recognizes and supports cricket but also provides us access to the artificial soccer pitch for regular practice sessions. In the 2023–2024 season, our team clinched the title in the Inter-University League Tournament—a moment of pride and celebration for all of us.
