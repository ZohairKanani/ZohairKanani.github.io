My Cooking Journey

Creating Culinary Delights

Cooking was never something I was passionate about—until I arrived at HKUST. With limited halal food options on campus, I started preparing my own meals out of necessity. Over time, it turned into something I genuinely enjoy.

There's something satisfying about choosing your own ingredients and experimenting with different dishes. Since I'm not cooking for a whole family like many mothers do, I'm not too precise with measurements—and yes, I mess up quite a bit. But that's part of the fun.

On weekends and holidays, I often cook with friends, and it's become one of my favorite hobbies. It's not just about the food—it's about the shared experience and the laughs along the way.
