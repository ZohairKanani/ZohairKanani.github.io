My Squash Journey

From Casual Player to University Team Member

I started playing squash two years ago after coming to HKUST. Initially, I started playing with my friends, but gradually practiced alone, which really boosted my skills and overall game.

I also randomly met the university team players practicing and joined with them to practice specific shots and further improve my game. As a result, I was able to get into the university team and I have been a member since last year.

The picture is from the tournament against the University of Macao, where we lost the overall competition but won the squash matches.
