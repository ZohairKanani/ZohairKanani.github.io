My Cycling Expeditions

Pedaling Through Adventures

Cycling is an activity I picked up after moving to Hong Kong, and it quickly became one of my favorite ways to explore the city. From challenging mountain trails to peaceful waterfront routes, Hong Kong offers a diverse range of biking experiences that are both thrilling and scenic.

One of the things I love most is how convenient it is to rent a bike—hourly rentals are widely available, and you don't need to return the bike to the same location you picked it up from. This flexibility makes spontaneous rides and long-distance tours incredibly easy.

I don't usually take many photos, but this one captures a great moment with a friend as we enjoyed one of Hong Kong's beautiful cycling paths near Sha Tin.
